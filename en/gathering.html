Gathering Details

Notice

  1. 1111特會是免費參加，但為安全因素，需在門口報到領取名牌，進出必須掛名牌;
  2. 特會沒有兒童看顧，家長必須看管自己的孩子，不要影響聚會的進行;
  3. 會場不准攜帶外食（除工作人員的工作飯盒以外），所以現場不會有微波爐給大家打熱食物，特會期間的用餐可以在會場外面的速食店或是不遠的Surrey Center解決;
  4. 聚會的時間，每天開始時間是固定，但結束時間不一定，有時甚至可能因為聖靈帶領就兩場連在一起，有時可能前一場延長了，就取消下一場也說不一定，請大家保持一個順服和喜樂的心，來跟隨聖靈和大會的帶領，保持一個彈性。謝謝大家的注意和配合。

Transportation

Venue: Aria Convention Center

Address: 12350 Pattullo Pl. Surrey, BC V3V 8C3. Canada

Transportation will NOT be provided from or to airport. You can take Sky Train, Taxi, or other means of transportation to get to Aria Convention Centre.

Vancouver International Airport（YVR）-> Aria Convention Centre

  1. From YVR Airport take Canada Line to Waterfront Station
  2. From Waterfront Station take Expo Line to King George, Get off at Scott Road Station.
  3. Walk from Scott Road Station to Aria Convention Centre is about 7 minutes. (600m away)

Public Transportation:
http://tripplanning.translink.ca/

Car Rental:
http://www.yvr.ca/en/passengers/transportation/car-rentals

Parking:

  • Please carpool or take public transportation
  • Convention Centre provide free parking
