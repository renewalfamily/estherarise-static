Donation

Hearing God

The 1111 Gathering is not a conference but a journey of all those of like heart who gather together and follow Him heart. We believe God richly bless those who hear and obey Him. Our blessing does not come from attending meetings but from hearing God and obeying what He asks of us.

We also believe hearing God is not a solo exercise; God wants us to walk as a body and as a family. Ask others you trust in the Lord - parents, family, spiritual leaders, friends - to pray and discern with you about coming to the gathering. Wisdom is found in a multitude of counsel and God will confirm His word not only in your own heart, but in the hearts of those who have a genuine love for Him and for you.

God wants us to simplify things, so there is no registration or fee for the gathering. We encourage you to listen to His voice. No matter where you are, you can still sow into this gathering through an offering.

Sowing Seed

The Lord challenged us that He wanted us to walk in the kingdom principle of sowing and reaping for this gathering. So rather than a registration fee, we are asking each person to pray and ask the Lord what He would have him sow into the gathering as seed. Some of you God will ask to sow a one-time gift; others He will speak to you to sow monthly for a period of time. Regardless of the amount or frequency, the important thing is that you honestly seek God and be obedient to whatever He tells you.

Although all of the coworkers are willing volunteers and lovers of God, this Gathering will cost more than Can$12,000 to cover for venue, sound and media, and administration. We believe God wants us to invest in His invisible kingdom and to believe the returns are greater than what we can imagine. “A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over will be poured into your lap!”

You can make your donation at the Gathering or send us a cheque by mail:
